In an ever changing work environment, Vanguard Technologists provide a Team of independent professionals to develop your residential project to the best standards on the market. Working with a number of independent Clients including Architects and Contractors, we provide technical solutions and drawings to ensure clarity both in design and onsite.

About us

Based in England and with over 30 years combined experience within the residential sector we appreciate the need for consistent quality and accuracy on technical drawing packs.

Over the last couple of years we noticed a need for professional, technical staff and business marred with economic uncertainty. With many smaller architectural businesses not being able to find the quality of expertise required to keep standards high or unable to guarantee long term options for those lucky few, we decided to set up a business to assist on a project by project basis nationwide.

With positive feedback across the board we have continued this success by careful delivery of projects, either at key stages or full Tender, as an outsourcing option.

Ethos

Our ethos is simple: To ensure the propagation of clear, accurate and cohesive technical drawing packs to mitigate confusion at design, tender or onsite. Through our skillset and knowledge base we believe we can provide and uplift drawing packs to provide this.
